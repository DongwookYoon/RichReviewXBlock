Speech synthesis is disabled in your browser setting

To enable your browser's speech synthesis engine, please take the following steps:

  1. Update your FireFox to the latest version.

  2. Type "about:config" in the address bar. Click "I accept the risk!" to proceed.

  3. Search "synth", spot "media.webspeech.synth.enabled", and double click it to set its value to "true".

  4. Start over from the edX.org.

If nothing helps, please contact the manager (dy252@cornell.edu).
